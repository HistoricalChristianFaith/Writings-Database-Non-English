Exposition on the Psalms of David - Editor's Note
==================================================

Editor's Note

The commonly received tradition of this exposition of Saint Thomas ended at Psalm 51. However, a catalogue compiled in 1473 attests that the original copy by Reginald of Piperno extended up to and including Psalm 54. In the course of the nineteenth century, Fr. A. Uccelli discovered in the Dominican convent at Naples a copy -- destroyed in 1943 -- containing the exposition of these three psalms that had been lost to the tradition, and he brought it to light. We have published its text separately.
